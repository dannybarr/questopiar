10 — Positioning

Why This Wins

How to differentiate from generic AI tools and existing VC software (Affinity, Visible, Carta) that incumbents are slow to adapt.

🎯
VC-Specific Training
Generic AI tools give generic answers. A system trained on VC memos, term sheets, and fund reporting knows that "strong team" means something very specific — and can output accordingly.
🔐
Data Moat
Every fund customer's anonymized data trains the model to be better for all customers. This is a network effect that becomes insurmountably hard to replicate after Year 2.
⚡
Workflow Integration
Affinity is a CRM. Carta is cap table software. Neither do AI research or generate memos. The opportunity is in the workflow gaps — the 80% of a VC analyst's day that isn't captured in any system.
🤝
Boutique → Platform
Start as a trusted research partner, not a SaaS vendor. VCs hire people they trust with their deal flow. Build the relationship first, then productize. Organic expansion beats cold sales in this industry.
